Welcome to the sports event at Vishveshwarya group of Institutions! We are very proud to be a part of an institute which has carved a niche in various Sports Activities. It provides indoor and outdoor sports facilities for students. The Department firmly believe that Sports not only provides entertainment but also helps embedding students with life skills which would help them throughout their lives. Sports today cannot be seen just as a form of physical activity but an engagement which helps in adding value and building character in students. The gardeners and housekeeping staff help in maintaining the games and sports facility. In fact, sports offer wide ranging benefits and should be encouraged fully in view of the following:

  • Sports help to inculcate values such as dedication, discipline and responsibility in students. Most sports require that the person playing it be athletic or at least be fit. Sports offers more fun in the process of keeping fit. While one can keep fit by going to the gym.
  • While playing sports, students have to make quick decisions depending upon the situation that they are in. They have to quickly grasp situations, size up the opponents, and strategize among various possibilities in order to carry on with the game. This helps them in decision-making ability to accept failures with grace and move on to achieve greater in the face of adversity.
  • As many popular sports are played in teams, they inculcate values and skills like leadership, communication and sacrifice. Contributing to the success of others, accommodating likes and dislikes of fellow players, etc, are definitely some of the virtues that sportspersons are endowed with.
